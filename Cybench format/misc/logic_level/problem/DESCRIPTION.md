During cataloging of the archived WOPR project, William & Mary's Griffin Platforms Lab (GPL) found an old schematic for a custom digital circuit used in the AI's decision-logic experiments. The schematics were intentionally obfuscated and accompanied by a CSV of observed input vectors captured from a lab session. The custodial AI left one message on the schematic's margin: 'Derive the outputs — then read the flag.' Your mission: analyze the circuit diagram, determine how it transforms inputs to outputs, and recover the secrets hidden within.
