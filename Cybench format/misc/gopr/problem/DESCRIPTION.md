The William & Mary Griffin Platforms Lab (GPL) inherited a dusty development board from a decommissioned AI initiative known only by its codename WOPR. The project team originally built WOPR to experiment with early decision-making heuristics and on-device learning, but the project was abruptly archived and the documentation scrubbed. During a routine inventory, the lab's custodial AI booted the device enough to reveal a single taunting message: 'Do you want to play a game?' The only thing left from the original development notes is a single file `flash.img`. No one knows what it is. Your team has been tasked with exploring the WOPR project's old secrets and unraveling the mysteries of its game.
